rebrand/strategy projects

project 1: MoodBloom app

moodbloom is a rebranding project focused on mental health awareness. I redesigned the visual identity to be more expressive, youthful, and emotionally impactful—using soft gradients and playful typography to resonate with young adults navigating their emotions.

my process

1. Research & Empathy Mapping

Identified target users and their emotional needs through surveys and interviews.

2. Brand Strategy

Developed a brand personality focused on warmth, empowerment, and approachability.

3. Visual Exploration

Created color palettes, logo sketches, and visual moodboards to guide the tone of the app.

4. UI Mockups

Designed high-fidelity screens in Figma with user-friendly layouts and calming aesthetics.

5. Prototype & Testing

Built a clickable prototype and gathered user feedback to refine and improve the design.
