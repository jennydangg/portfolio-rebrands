rebrand/strategy projects

project 1: MoodBloom app

MoodBloom is a mental health awareness app designed for college and university students looking to track their daily moods or manage emotional challenges. while it’s not a substitute for professional therapy, the app encourages mental wellness and self-reflection. i helped design the visual identity to feel expressive and youthful, using soft gradients and playful typography to connect with young adults navigating their emotions.

project 2: Roger's brand strategy

this strategic campaign for Rogers was developed to increase user engagement with the Rogers Beyond the Seat app. our team focused on creating a tiered rewards system that offered exclusive perks to loyal Rogers customers, such as early access to event tickets, behind-the-scenes content, and personalized offers. by making the experience more interactive and reward-driven, the campaign aimed to strengthen brand loyalty and encourage more consistent app usage. i contributed to the development of the campaign concept and visual strategy to ensure it aligned with Rogers’ brand identity while bringing in new customers.

project 3: Wet n Wild redesign

for this rebrand, i focused on modernizing Wet n Wild’s identity by introducing a softer color palette, rounded typography, and refreshed packaging design. the goal was to better align the brand with gen z consumers while keeping its playful and accessible personality.
